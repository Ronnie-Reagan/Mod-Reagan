Advantages of Mod Reagan

  • Cross-server tracking ensures accountability across communities.
  • Minimizes moderator bias with structured, role-based moderation.
  • Improves community safety by preventing rule-breakers from evading consequences.
  • Facilitates transparency in moderation decisions.

Why Moderators and Servers Benefit

Mod Reagan provides a robust framework for handling moderation. It empowers servers to:

  • Maintain independence with customizable laws and rules.
  • Collaborate with other servers to uphold shared values.
  • Strengthen trust within their communities.
